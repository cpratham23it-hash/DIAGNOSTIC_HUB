How it works

Three inputs. One verdict.

Most people already have the evidence — a weird noise, a leak, a smell. Diagnos knows how to read it.

01 — Capture

Photo, sound, or words

Snap the rust or the leak, record the rattle, or type what's happening. The phone already in your hand is enough.

02 — Read

The system reads the signal

Image models spot rust and damage. Audio models detect compressor strain, fan imbalance, bearing wear. Your words narrow it further.

03 — Decide

A straight answer

A ranked list of likely causes with confidence scores. A fair cost range for your city. And a technician if you need one — not before.

FAQ

Questions people actually ask.

Diagnos gives you a ranked list of likely causes with a confidence score — not one hard answer. In testing, the top result matched the actual fault in about 79% of cases when both audio and image were provided. With text only, closer to 63%. We show 45% confidence as 45%, not dressed up as 90%.

No. If you can describe what sounds or looks wrong, that's enough to start. The more you give — a photo, a short clip, a description — the sharper the result. But two sentences about the problem will get you a starting diagnosis.

The engine is currently tuned for refrigerators, ACs, washing machines, air purifiers, and security cameras. We're expanding. For anything else, the text-based symptom checker still gives useful starting points for most home appliances.

Photos and audio are processed for your diagnosis and stored against your appliance record. They are not shared, sold, or used to train any external model. You can delete any file or your entire account at any time. Full details in the privacy policy.

Estimates are built from a database of historical repair costs, broken down by fault type and city — the 25th–75th percentile of real job costs, not a manufacturer's suggested price. Enough to know if a quote is in the right range. Not a guarantee of what you'll pay.
